PROCESS FLOW

How we work

  • CONTACT

    Contact us with a reference footage and the details of what you need.

  • REVIEW

    Our team will review the footage and give you the time and cost along with any queries we have.

  • APPROVAL

    You can give us the greenlight if you like our proposal and send us the working plates.

  • WORK

    We'll start working on the shots and send you WIP versions for you to review.

  • PRODUCT

    After you approve the shots we'll send you the final product. You can contact us if you need any changes.

  • BILLING

    After everything is approved we'll invoice you with the cost agreed upon.
